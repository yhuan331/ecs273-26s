Title: You can still make purchases with your Spirit Airlines credit card
Date: 2026-05-04 18:50
URL: https://www.usatoday.com/story/money/2026/05/04/spirit-airlines-free-spirit-credit-card/89931545007/?.tsrc=rss

Full Summary Spirit Airlines credit cardholders will still be able to use their cards for purchases, even now that the airline hassuspended its flight operations. According to Bank of America, which issued the Free Spirit Travel Mastercard and the Free Spirit Travel More World Elite Mastercard, card accounts remain active. Don Vecchiarello, a Bank of America spokesperson, told USA TODAY that the issuer isn't accepting new Spirit credit card applications, but that existing cards will continue to function as normal – though purchases will no longer generate points in the defunct frequent flyer program. "Current Free Spirit accounts remain open and active and will continue to be serviced by Bank of America. Free Spirit cardholders can continue to use their card as normal for everyday purchases, and there is no immediate action required on their part," Vecchiarello said in an email. He added that Bank of America will reach out to cardholders about any account updates and said that some may be eligible to transition their accounts to other cards issued by the bank. "Bank of America remains committed to serving our Free Spirit cardholders as we work through this transition and will continue to keep them informed every step of the way," Vecchiarello said.
